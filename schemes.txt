PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana:
The PM Kisan scheme provides financial assistance of Rs 6000 per year to farmer families across India.
The amount is paid in three installments of Rs 2000 each.
Eligibility: Small and marginal farmers with cultivable land up to 2 hectares.
How to apply: Visit pmkisan.gov.in or nearest Common Service Centre.
Documents needed: Aadhaar card, land records, bank account details.

Karnataka Raitha Siri Scheme:
The Karnataka government provides Rs 2000 per acre per season to farmers.
Maximum benefit is Rs 10000 per year per farmer family.
Eligibility: Farmers who own land in Karnataka and are registered with the agriculture department.
How to apply: Visit nearest Raitha Samparka Kendra or apply online at karnataka.gov.in.
Documents needed: Land records, Aadhaar card, bank passbook.

PM Fasal Bima Yojana:
Crop insurance scheme that protects farmers against crop loss due to natural calamities.
Premium is very low - only 2% for kharif crops and 1.5% for rabi crops.
Eligibility: All farmers growing notified crops in notified areas.
How to apply: Contact nearest bank or insurance company before sowing season.
Documents needed: Land records, Aadhaar card, bank account, sowing certificate.

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Credit Card:
Provides short term credit to farmers for crop cultivation expenses.
Credit limit up to Rs 3 lakh at interest rate of 7% per year.
Interest subvention of 3% for prompt repayment making effective rate 4%.
Eligibility: All farmers including tenant farmers and sharecroppers.
How to apply: Visit nearest bank branch with land records.
Documents needed: Aadhaar card, land records, passport photo, bank account.

Karnataka Krishi Yantra Dhare Scheme:
Provides farm machinery on rental basis to small and marginal farmers.
Tractors, tillers, harvesters available at subsidized rental rates.
Eligibility: Small farmers with less than 2 hectares of land in Karnataka.
How to apply: Visit nearest Raitha Samparka Kendra.
Documents needed: Land records, Aadhaar card, farmer ID.

PM Kisan Tractor Subsidy Scheme:
Provides 20% to 50% subsidy on purchase of new tractors.
Maximum subsidy amount is Rs 1.5 lakh per farmer.
Eligibility: Farmers who do not already own a tractor, land holding up to 5 acres.
How to apply: Apply through state agriculture department or dealer.
Documents needed: Land records, Aadhaar card, bank account, quotation from dealer.

Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana RKVY:
Central government scheme for development of agriculture and allied sectors.
Provides funds for farm infrastructure, storage, processing units.
Eligibility: Farmers and farmer groups in all states.
How to apply: Contact district agriculture officer.
Documents needed: Land records, project proposal, bank account.

Karnataka Bhagyalakshmi Scheme:
Financial assistance for girl child born in below poverty line families including farmers.
Rs 10000 fixed deposit at birth, matures to Rs 1 lakh at age 18.
Eligibility: BPL families in Karnataka with girl child born after April 2006.
How to apply: Apply at nearest Anganwadi center within one year of birth.
Documents needed: Birth certificate, BPL card, Aadhaar card, bank account.

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana Gramin:
Housing scheme for rural poor including farmers without pucca house.
Financial assistance of Rs 1.2 lakh in plain areas and Rs 1.3 lakh in hilly areas.
Eligibility: Homeless families and families living in kutcha houses in rural areas.
How to apply: Apply through Gram Panchayat or visit pmayg.nic.in.
Documents needed: Aadhaar card, bank account, BPL certificate.

Soil Health Card Scheme:
Free soil testing for farmers to know nutrients in their land.
Provides recommendations for fertilizers based on soil test results.
Eligibility: All farmers across India.
How to apply: Contact nearest Krishi Vigyan Kendra or agriculture department.
Documents needed: Land records, Aadhaar card.

National Food Security Mission:
Increases production of rice, wheat, pulses and coarse cereals.
Provides seeds, fertilizers and training to farmers at subsidized rates.
Eligibility: Farmers in identified districts across India.
How to apply: Contact district agriculture officer.
Documents needed: Land records, farmer registration certificate.

Karnataka Organic Farming Scheme:
Promotes organic farming in Karnataka with financial incentives.
Rs 10000 per hectare per year for converting to organic farming.
Eligibility: Farmers willing to adopt organic farming for minimum 3 years.
How to apply: Register at nearest Raitha Samparka Kendra.
Documents needed: Land records, Aadhaar card, commitment letter.

PM Kusum Scheme:
Solar pump scheme for farmers to reduce dependence on grid electricity.
90% subsidy on solar pumps for irrigation.
Eligibility: All farmers with agricultural land and water source.
How to apply: Apply through state nodal agency or discom.
Documents needed: Land records, Aadhaar card, water source proof.

Kisan Vikas Patra:
Investment scheme where money doubles in 115 months.
Available at post offices across India.
Minimum investment Rs 1000, no maximum limit.
Eligibility: Any Indian citizen including farmers.
How to apply: Visit nearest post office.
Documents needed: Aadhaar card, PAN card for investment above Rs 50000.

Karnataka Sericulture Scheme:
Financial assistance for silkworm rearing and mulberry cultivation.
Subsidy up to Rs 30000 per hectare for mulberry plantation.
Eligibility: Farmers in Karnataka interested in silk farming.
How to apply: Contact Department of Sericulture Karnataka.
Documents needed: Land records, Aadhaar card, bank account.

National Horticulture Mission:
Promotes cultivation of fruits, vegetables and flowers.
Subsidy on drip irrigation, poly houses, cold storage.
Eligibility: Farmers growing horticultural crops.
How to apply: Contact district horticulture officer.
Documents needed: Land records, project proposal, bank account.

Karnataka Drought Relief Scheme:
Compensation to farmers for crop loss due to drought.
Rs 6800 per hectare for irrigated crops and Rs 3400 for dryland crops.
Eligibility: Farmers in drought declared taluks in Karnataka.
How to apply: Apply through village accountant or Gram Panchayat.
Documents needed: Land records, crop loss certificate, Aadhaar card, bank account.

Pradhan Mantri Micro Food Processing Scheme:
Financial assistance for setting up small food processing units.
Credit linked subsidy of 35% up to Rs 10 lakh.
Eligibility: Individual farmers and farmer groups wanting to process farm produce.
How to apply: Apply through state nodal agency or visit mofpi.gov.in.
Documents needed: Land records, project report, bank account, Aadhaar card.

E Nam National Agriculture Market:
Online trading platform connecting farmers directly to buyers.
Farmers get better prices by selling directly without middlemen.
Eligibility: All farmers registered with their APMC.
How to apply: Register at enam.gov.in or nearest APMC office.
Documents needed: Aadhaar card, bank account, APMC registration.

Karnataka Raitha Bandhu Scheme:
Zero interest crop loans up to Rs 3 lakh for farmers in Karnataka.
No interest charged for loans repaid within one year.
Eligibility: Farmers with land records in Karnataka registered with cooperative bank.
How to apply: Visit nearest District Cooperative Central Bank.
Documents needed: Land records, Aadhaar card, bank passbook, farmer ID card.
